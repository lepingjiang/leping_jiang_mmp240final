Computer Enthusiast (Magazine Design)

2019

Medium: Adobe InDesign

A project for a multimedia design class for which I used Adobe InDesign to make a magazine cover and magazine spread. Legibility and hierarchy of information were important features.

I had two topics in mind: gaming and computers. In the end, I chose computers because at the time there was more exciting news around the subject.

I wanted to suggest a retro look. I played with the colors and layout before coming up with the final version.
