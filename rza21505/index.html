RZA21505-OAE (Bitmap Font)

2019

Medium: Adobe Illustrator

A project for a multimedia design class. I used Adobe Illustrator, to make a decorative font with 1:1 square.

A lot of bitmap fonts look very similar, and I wanted my design to be different. I looked at a lot of fonts and found none that were based on outlining shapes. This is the reason I chose this approach.
