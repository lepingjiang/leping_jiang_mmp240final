2019

Medium: Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator

A project for a multimedia programming class using Adobe XD. I had to work with a partner to come up with an idea for an app for students. We chose tutoring and books as our topic and named the project “Studybook Network”

The app features tutoring for student and allows them to sell and buy textbooks. The app has a light and dark mode.
